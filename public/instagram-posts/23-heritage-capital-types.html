The Framework

Heritage is cross-dimensional capital

Human Capital
Values & Skills
System of values, solidarity of residents, craftsmanship passed between generations
Social Capital
Bonds & Trust
Regional customs, folk traditions, cooperation networks that strengthen community resilience
Economic Capital
Assets & Tourism
Historic infrastructure, cultural sites, and material heritage as tourism destinations
Ecological Capital
Land & Knowledge
Respect for natural resources, traditional agriculture, environmental stewardship

"Cultural heritage is not only a marker of identity but a key resource for promoting socio-economic and environmental sustainability."

Source: Musialik, W. et al. (2025). "Cultural heritage capital as a resilience factor in sustainable development policy of the region." Economics and Environment.
