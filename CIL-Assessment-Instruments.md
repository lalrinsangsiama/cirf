# Cultural Innovation Lab (CIL) Platform — Assessment Instruments

**Total: 6 assessments, 151 questions (17 demographic + 134 Likert-scale)**

**Response Scale (all Likert items):** 7-point scale from 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 7 (Strongly Agree). Some items allow a "Not Applicable" response. Reverse-scored items are marked with **(R)**.

---

## Assessment 1: CIL — Cultural Innovation Lab Assessment (Primary)

**61 questions (17 demographic + 44 Likert) | 5 sections | ~20 minutes | Free**

This is the foundational assessment. Completing it unlocks access to the five secondary assessments.

---

### Section A: Context & Demographics (17 questions)

These are not Likert items. They collect respondent profile information using text fields, dropdowns, and multi-select checkboxes.

1. **Name** (optional, text)
2. **Business or initiative name** (optional, text)
3. **Website** (optional, text)
4. **Instagram username** (optional, text)
5. **Age Group** (select: 18-25, 26-35, 36-45, 46-55, 56+)
6. **Gender** (select: Female, Male, Non-binary, Prefer not to say)
7. **Country** (select: 60+ countries)
8. **State/Province/Region** (text)
9. **Community or cultural heritage** (optional, text — e.g., Mizo, Maori, Yoruba, Navajo Nation, Tamil, Sami, Igbo, Quechua, Basque, Balinese, Okinawan)
10. **What type of organization are you assessing?** (select: Cooperative/Social Enterprise, Community Organization/NGO, Indigenous Enterprise, Cultural Institution/Museum, Craft Guild/Artisan Collective, For-Profit Cultural Business, Government/Public Agency, Individual Cultural Entrepreneur, Other)
11. **Which cultural sectors are you active in?** (multi-select: Crafts & Artisanal Products, Performing Arts, Visual Arts & Photography, Food & Culinary Traditions, Fashion & Textiles, Cultural & Heritage Tourism, Digital Content/Film/Media, AR/VR/XR & Immersive Experiences, Gaming & Interactive Media, Mobility & Cultural Transport, Language/Literature/Storytelling, Festivals/Ceremonies/Events, Traditional Medicine & Wellness, Design & Creative Services, Cultural Education, Community Cultural Development, Cultural Agriculture & Farming, Other)
12. **What stage is your initiative at?** (select: Idea/Concept Stage, Startup 0-2 years, Growth Stage 2-5 years, Scaling 5-10 years, Established 10+ years)
13. **How many people are directly involved in the initiative?** (select: Solo/Individual, 2-5 people, 6-10, 11-25, 26-50, 51+)
14. **How many total employees does your organization have?** (select: None/just me, 1-5, 6-15, 16-30, 31-50, 51-100, 100+)
15. **What is your approximate annual revenue/budget?** (select: Pre-revenue, <$1,000, $1,000-$5,000, $5,000-$10,000, $10,000-$25,000, $25,000-$50,000, $50,000-$100,000, $100,000-$500,000, $500,000+)
16. **What are your main sources of revenue?** (multi-select: Direct sales of cultural products, Services/performances/workshops, Tourism-related activities, Digital content monetization, Licensing/royalties, Grants/donations, Teaching/training, Other)
17. **What type of economic disruption has your enterprise experienced?** (multi-select: Pandemic, Economic recession/downturn, Natural disaster, Political instability or conflict, Major market shift or new competition, Supply chain disruption, Climate-related disruption, No significant disruption experienced)
