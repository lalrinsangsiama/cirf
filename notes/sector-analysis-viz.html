Key Sectoral Insights

  • Digital Paradox: Digital sectors show highest economic success (10.5 avg score) but face cultural authenticity challenges
  • Craft Crisis: Traditional crafts have highest cultural integrity (95%) but lowest adaptive capacity (45%)
  • Culinary Sweet Spot: Food enterprises achieve optimal balance with 85% success rate
  • Adaptation Gap: 40-50% difference in adaptive capacity between digital and traditional sectors
  • Hybrid Advantage: Mixed sector approaches show 20-30% higher resilience scores
