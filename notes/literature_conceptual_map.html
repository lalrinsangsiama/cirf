PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION

Cultural Extraction Paradox

UNESCO (2022), Throsby (2001), Comaroff & Comaroff (2009)
$2.25 trillion cultural economy with minimal community benefit. Systematic commodification of culture as "Ethnicity, Inc."

Biopiracy & Appropriation

Shiva (2001), Brown (2003), Scafidi (2005)
$43 billion pharmaceutical profits from traditional knowledge. Legal frameworks enable systematic appropriation.

Development Failure

Ferguson (1990), Escobar (2018), Scott (1998)
False tradition/modernity binary. Technical solutions ignore structural extraction. High-modernist schemes destroy local knowledge.

Creative Industries Critique

Hesmondhalgh & Pratt (2005), McRobbie (2016), Peck (2005)
Creative industries reproduce inequalities. Mask precarious conditions behind entrepreneurship rhetoric.
THEORETICAL FOUNDATIONS

Innovation Theory

Schumpeter (1934), Von Hippel (2005)
Creative destruction vs. user innovation. Foundation for "protective innovation" alternative.

Resilience Theory

Holling (1973), Folke et al. (2010), Berkes (2012)
Ecological resilience principles. Traditional knowledge as resilience resource. Multi-dimensional capacities.

Community Control

Ostrom (1990), Chambers (1997), Smith (2012)
Common pool resource governance. Participatory development. Decolonizing methodologies.

Economic Geography

Ray (1998), Storper (1997), Harvey (2001)
Local assets as development foundation. Non-tradeable competitive advantages. Accumulation by dispossession.

Research Methodology

Mertens (2010), Israel et al. (2012), Creswell & Plano Clark (2017)
Transformative paradigm. Community-based participatory research. Mixed methods framework development.
